Executive Summary & Glossary

Executive Summary

This archive documents 41 episodes of the "Conspiracy Theories Real or Fake w/ Facts" podcast, hosted by Brother O.T.G., spanning a significant 188-day period from February 20, 2021, to August 27, 2021. The collection represents over 248 hours of in-depth discussions on a wide range of topics, from historical and political conspiracies to social issues and esoteric knowledge. The podcast provided a unique "Black First" perspective on these subjects, fostering a space for late-night conversations and truth-seeking. This period is regarded by many listeners as life-changing, marked by its intense schedule and the depth of its content.

Glossary of Key Terms

  • CTRFWF: Abbreviation for "Conspiracy Theories Real or Fake w/ Facts."
  • O.T.G: Over Time Grind, the brand and philosophy of the host.
  • Safe Spaces: A term for the live rooms on Clubhouse where the podcast was recorded, emphasizing a "Black First" environment for open discussion.
  • Project C.T.R.L: A series within the podcast focusing on specific deep-dive topics like MKUltra and historical timelines.
  • Tin Foil Hat Time: A recurring segment or series dedicated to more speculative and esoteric conspiracy theories.
